About Jahnavihub

A student-driven hub for sharing, learning, and growing together.

📘 Our Purpose

Jahnavihub is a student-initiated platform dedicated to preserving and sharing valuable college resources — from notes and study materials to event archives and shared experiences. It's designed to help juniors access everything in one place.

🧠 Our Vision

To build a digital archive where knowledge, memories, and updates continue to grow batch by batch. We believe in a simple idea: learn together, grow stronger together.
